# Content Strategy

Framework for planning, producing, and distributing communications content.

## Content Types

| Type | Audience | Cadence | Owner |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| Press release | Media / public | As needed | Communications Lead |
| Executive blog post | Customers / public | Monthly | Communications Lead + CEO/CTO |
| Internal newsletter | Employees | Bi-weekly | Communications Lead |
| Product announcement | Customers / partners | Per release | Communications Lead + PM |
| Investor update | Investors | Quarterly | Communications Lead + Finance |
| All-hands deck | Employees | Monthly | Communications Lead + CEO |

## Content Production Workflow

1. **Brief** — Define audience, objective, key messages, and deadline
2. **Draft** — Write first draft; follow brand voice guidelines
3. **Review** — Share with relevant stakeholders (Legal for external; execs for announcements)
4. **Revise** — Incorporate feedback in one consolidated round
5. **Approve** — Get final sign-off from designated approver
6. **Publish / distribute** — Schedule or send via appropriate channels
7. **Measure** — Track open rates, coverage, or sentiment as applicable

## Brand Voice Guidelines

- **Clear**: Use plain language; avoid acronyms without explanation
- **Confident**: State positions directly; avoid weasel words ("may", "might", "could potentially")
- **Human**: Write for a person, not a legal document
- **Consistent**: Same terminology across all channels (maintain a glossary in `memory/MEMORY.md`)

## Press Release Structure

```markdown
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[HEADLINE — active voice, present tense, newsworthy]

[SUBHEADLINE — additional context or key benefit]

[City, Date] — [Company] today announced [key news in first paragraph — who, what, why it matters].

[Second paragraph — context, how it works, significance]

[Quote from executive — perspective, not repetition of facts]

[Third paragraph — supporting details, metrics, availability]

[Boilerplate — standard company description]

###

Media contact:
[Name], Communications Lead
[Email] | [Phone]
```

## Announcement Checklist

- [ ] Key message is clear in the first sentence
- [ ] Audience benefit is explicit
- [ ] Supporting evidence or data included
- [ ] Executive quote adds genuine insight (not just filler)
- [ ] Call to action is clear
- [ ] Legal / compliance reviewed (for external)
- [ ] Distribution channels confirmed
- [ ] Embargo or timing coordinated with relevant teams
